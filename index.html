The Process

Five Steps to Bone Health Clarity

A simple, non-invasive process for early osteoporosis detection.

1

Wear Band

The user wears a BP-style sensor band on the arm to start screening.

2

Collect Signals

Sensors inside the band collect body signals related to bone strength and movement response.

3

Transmit Data

The signals are sent through a wired connection to a display unit.

4

AI Analysis

An AI system analyzes the data and calculates osteoporosis risk.

5

Instant Results

The device shows a bone health score and risk level for early detection and prevention.
